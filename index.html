What is VIZ!!

While developing a Machine learning models , one should understand the basic mathematics behind it , For facilitating that "VIZ", A platform to visualsize the machine learning models to understand its working and functionalities


Importance of Model visualisation :

  • Debugging the undesirable errors in the model
  • Comparison of models
  • Useful for explaining the models for common user during proposal
  • Easy interpretation of results of projects.
